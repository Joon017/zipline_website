How it works

We watch the source, so you don't have to.

No middlemen, no aggregator-of-aggregators. Zipline reads the real career pages and keeps them in sync.

01

We monitor career pages

Our scrapers watch each company's official careers page around the clock — the same page you'd visit yourself.

02

We aggregate & normalize

Every role is parsed into a clean, consistent format — title, team, location, and a link back to apply directly.

03

You browse one directory

Search and filter thousands of openings by company, title, or country — then apply on the company's own site.

Why Zipline

The most reliable source is the company itself.

Straight from the source

Every listing is pulled directly from the employer's own career page — the single most trustworthy place a job can live.

Always fresh, never stale

We re-check pages every day. When a role closes on the company site, it disappears here too — no dead links.

No reposts or ghost jobs

Because we skip the job boards entirely, you won't find duplicate reposts, expired listings, or roles that were never real.
