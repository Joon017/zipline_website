Frequently asked questions

What is Zipline Jobs?

Zipline is a job directory that pulls open roles directly from company career pages. Instead of relying on what companies choose to post on job boards, we monitor the source and surface listings as they appear.

How is this different from LinkedIn or Indeed?

Job boards depend on companies actively posting to them, which means listings are often delayed, incomplete, or missing entirely. Zipline reads career pages directly, so you see what's actually open rather than what's been submitted to a third party.

Which companies are tracked?

We currently track 180+ companies across 20+ sectors, from early-stage startups to large enterprises, and we're constantly expanding coverage. We support direct career pages as well as all major ATS providers like Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and Workday. You can also suggest companies you want us to add.

How often are listings updated?

The directory is refreshed daily. When a company posts or closes a role on their career page, it reflects in Zipline within 24 hours.

Is it free?

Yes. Zipline is free to use, with no account required to browse. We want the directory to be as accessible as possible.

How do I suggest a company to track?

You can submit a company and their career page URL through the Submit a company page. We review submissions and usually have them live within a few days.
